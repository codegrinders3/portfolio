AutoMath

OCR based Android application.

There are lot of calculation based applications available in the android market. Our application goes one step further in helping users to calculate expressions even faster. Automath uses OCR technology to capture and convert image of the expression from mobile's camera into text and display the output on the screen at the same time. It also display steps of solving expression for learning purposes, solves linear equations, solving complex math functions quickly such as exponents and logarithms, etc.
